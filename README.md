#Electron apps
This repository builds three seperate electron apps:
 - The OmnomCom app
 - The ThreeLittlePigs app
 - The Donkey App

What these display and are built with are defined according to their own config files.
They are built for both windows and linux and make use of their respective auto update functionality via the automated github releases.

## OmnomCom App
The OmnomCom app displays the OmnomCom store in ProtoPolis on Gingy.

## The Three Little Pigs App
Three Little Pigs is the slightly rewritten successor to Hydra, and is the Electron application hosting the three main screens of the Protopolis and SmartXP: ProTube, the narrowcasting and the overview screen on the Three Little Pigs server.

## The Donkey App
Donkey is almost the same as the Three Little Pigs, but it only has two screens. It shows the overview screen and ProTube on Donkey.
